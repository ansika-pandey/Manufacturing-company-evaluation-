# Manufacturing Company Evaluation

## Overview
This project evaluates Indian manufacturing companies using a structured business evaluation framework. Each company is assessed based on eligibility, technical differentiation, leadership quality, market tailwinds, growth signals, systems maturity, and leadership succession.

## Objective
- Research manufacturing companies using public sources.
- Apply a consistent scoring framework.
- Identify high-potential companies based on operational and business criteria.

## Evaluation Framework
### Eligibility Gates
- E1: Producer
- E2: Accessible

### Scoring Criteria
- C3: Differentiation
- C4: Decision-Maker Quality
- C5: Growing Sector
- C6: Growth Signals
- C7: Systems Maturity
- C8: Leadership Succession
